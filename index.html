Vinayak Kumar

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at UT Austin in theoretical computer science, currently working with David Zuckerman. I am interested in complexity theory, pseudorandomness, and the design/analysis of various algorithms.

Before joining UT Austin in 2021, I received my B.S. at Caltech in math and computer science. During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to be mentored by Venkatesan Guruswami and Leonard Schulman.
